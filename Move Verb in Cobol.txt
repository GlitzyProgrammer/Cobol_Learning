Move Verb

Limits 
	Source and Destination can be either elementary or group data items 
	
	When "moved" what ever is in the destination is overwritten and source is undisturbed
	
	If the number of characters in the source item is too few then the destination would fill it with zeros
	
	Truncation takes place if the opposite problem occurs 
	
	When the destination item is alphanumeric or alphabetic data is copied in the destination area from
left to right with space filling or truncation on the right.
	
	When the destination item is numeric or edited numeric data is aligned along the decimal point with zero-filling or truncation as needed.
